  • There is Still Time to Enroll

    As your payment processor, Sterling Payment Technologies, LLC has partnered with MCAG, a class action settlement expert, to streamline the filing process and secure recoveries for our valued merchants from the "Visa®/MasterCard® Interchange Fee Class Action Settlement.” The lawsuit claims that merchants paid more fees than they should have for accepting Visa® and MasterCard® as payment from consumers during the period between January 1, 2004 and November 28, 2012.
